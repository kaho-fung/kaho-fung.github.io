Manual of Kaho 📕

Conditions I like to work in 🏢

💻 + 🎧 + ☕/🧋/🥤 + 🤫 = 👌

I prefer and value focused time. I like working in a place which does not give me too many distractions, while keeping myself motivated. For this reason I mostly work in a quiet co-working space. To maintain confidentiality of my work, I would have my calls in private meeting rooms/ telephone booths if required. You can also tell me before you start mentioning anything sensitive.

Times/ Hours I like to work 🕐

8.30am - 5.15pm UK time. I sometimes stay late for a little longer to catch up on emails and messages I haven't looked at/ replied, that may be due to I spent some time mid-day with my family (they're in GMT+8 timezone and I struggle to find time to talk to them).

My usual in-zone time is between 2pm to 5pm.

Best way to communicate with me 🗣️

I appreciate connecting over Teams for a quick call but not directly calling me - I need some brain capacity to process and be informed of what needs to be discussed!

Text > Phone Call > Email. I generally read e-mail the same day, but my inbox is not a chat tool or a to-do list.

Please block my calendar if you require my input for more than 10 minutes.

The way I like to receive feedback 💬

I love all kinds of feedback, but would be appreciated you keep them private if they are really direct and blunt. I am more than happy to go through your concerns (and provide solutions) 1:1.

If I don't respond to you... 💤

Trust me, it isn't because I dislike you. It's probably becuase your questions are somewhat challenging. Feel free to send me a chaser or 10 minutes call on Teams.

Things I love 😻

Honesty, transparency and being organised and well-prepared.

Things I need 🫂

Trust, space and time to think (i am a very slow thinker, hence a disappointing person to try to debate or attack).
