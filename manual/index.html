Manual of Kaho 📕

Conditions I like to work in 🏢

💻 + 🎧 + ☕/🧋/🥤 + 🤫 = 👌

I prefer and value focused time. I enjoy working in an environment that minimises distractions while keeping me motivated. For this reason, I primarily work in a quiet co-working space. To maintain the confidentiality of my work, I conduct calls in private meeting rooms or telephone booths when necessary. You can also tell me before discussing any sensitive matters.

Times/ Hours I work 🕐

8.30am - 5.15pm UK time. Occassionally I stay a bit later to catch up on emails and messages that I haven't had a chance to address, especially when I spend time with my family (they're in GMT+8 timezone) around midday.

My usual in-zone hours are typically between 2pm to 5pm.

Best way to communicate with me 🗣️

I appreciate connecting over Teams for a quick call but please avoid directly calls - I need some brain capacity to process and be informed of what needs to be discussed!

In terms of communication priority: Text > Phone Call > Email. I generally read e-mail the same day, but my inbox is not a chat tool or a to-do list.

If you need more than 10 minutes of my input, please block my calendar accordingly.

How I prefer to receive feedback 💬

I love all forms of feedback, but I would appreciate it if you keep really really direct and blunt comment private. I am more than willing to address your concerns (and provide solutions) 1:1.

If I don't respond to you... 💤

Rest assured, it's not because I dislike you. It's probably becuase your questions are challenging. If you require a more immediate response please feel free to send me a chaser or schedule a 10-minutes call on Teams.

Things I love 😻

Honesty, transparency and being organised and well-prepared. My aim is to check all these boxes.

Things I need 🫂

Trust, personal space and time to think (i am a very slow thinker, which can make me appear unresponsive during debates and discussions).

What Kaho thinks he's good at 💪

Very eager to learn anything new. Good listener. 👍 at ingesting, organising complex information and giving concise summaries.

What Kaho thinks he's not so good at 😰

Communicating the issues I am facing (without thorough preparation). Additionally I am not very talkative and my social awkwardness = 3000.
