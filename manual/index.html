Manual of Kaho 📕

So... who is Kaho?

I am a Cyber Security Risk Manager. As the role says I talk a lot about cyber risk, and also what do we do to manage them to an acceptable level.

My objective of work is to enable the decision makers in the organisation to select the best option when they're dealing matters around cyber.

Conditions I like to work in 🏢

💻 + 🎧 + ☕/🧋/🥤 + 🤫 = 👌

I prefer and value focused time. I enjoy working in an environment that minimises distractions while keeping me motivated. For this reason, I primarily work in a quiet co-working space. To maintain the confidentiality of my work, I conduct calls in private meeting rooms or telephone booths when necessary. You can also tell me before discussing any sensitive matters.

Times/ Hours I work 🕐

I usually work 8.30am - 5.15pm UK time. I would occassionally start (and finish) my day earlier as I like to go out for a run while sun is still out - UK winter can be very daunting!

My lunch time is usually spent to catch up with my family (They reside in Asia and there is 7-8 hours of time difference so lunch is a very good timing for all of us.)

My usual in-zone hours are typically between 1pm to 4pm.

Best way to communicate with me 🗣️

I appreciate connecting over Teams for a quick call but please avoid directly calling - I need some brain capacity to process and be informed of what needs to be discussed! If you need more than 10 minutes of my input, please block my calendar accordingly.

I understand pepole think about work even when they are not at work, and if a good idea isn't told/ written down it will be forgotten - so feel free to text/ email me outside business hours.

In terms of communication priority: Text ➡️ Phone Call ➡️ Email. I generally read e-mail the same day, but my inbox is not a chat tool or a to-do list.

I regard my written responses much better than verbal as I am a slow thinker I struggle to come up with good idea by talking straight out of my mind.

How I prefer to receive feedback 💬

I love all forms of feedback, but I would appreciate it if you keep really really direct and blunt comment private. I am more than willing to address your concerns (and provide solutions) 1:1.

If I don't respond to you... 💤

Rest assured, it's not because I dislike you. It's probably becuase your questions are challenging. If you require a more immediate response please feel free to send me a chaser on Teams or schedule a 10-minutes call.

Things I love 😻

Honesty, transparency and being organised and well-prepared. My aim is to check all these boxes.

Oh, and I love cats.

Things I need 🫂

Trust, personal space and time to think (i am a very slow thinker, which can make me appear unresponsive during debates and discussions).

What Kaho thinks he's good at 💪

Very eager to learn anything new. Good listener. 👍 at ingesting, organising complex information and giving concise summaries.
