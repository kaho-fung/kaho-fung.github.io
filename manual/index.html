👋 Manual of Kaho 📕

Last updated in January 2024

 

What is this?
Manual of me is a powerful way to facilitate mutual understanding among team members. In every organisation, there are diverse personalities, perspectives and work styles. This manual aims to help you understand how I prefer to communicate and work. If you find this useful, consider writing your own!

 

So... who is Kaho?

I am a Cyber Security Risk Manager. As the role says I talk a lot about cyber risk, and also to advise how could we manage them to an acceptable level.

I am specialised in risk quantification, a way to measure risk in monetary terms.

My objective of work is to enable senior management in the organisation to select the best option when they're dealing matters around cyber.

Times/ Hours I work 🕐

I usually work 8.30am - 5.15pm UK time. I try to stick to this schedule most of the time but I may start or finish earlier/ later if I have personal commitments (exercise, family etc).

My lunch time is usually spent to catch up with my family. They reside in Asia and there is 7/8 hours of time difference.

My usual in-zone hours are typically between 1pm to 4pm. During this period I usually set my status to Do Not Disturb.

Environment I like to work in 🏢

💻 + 🎧 + {☕/🧋/🥤} + 🤫 = 👍

I usually spend my time in a co-working space close to my home as I enjoy working in an environment with little distractions.

To maintain the confidentiality of my work, I conduct calls in private meeting rooms or telephone booths when necessary. You can also tell me before discussing any sensitive matters.

Best way to communicate with me 🗣️

  • Avoid direct calling
    • While I appreciate connecting over Teams for quick call, I need some brain capacity to process and be informed of what needs to be discussed. Please before you call.
  • My email inbox is not a chat tool
    • I generally read e-mail the same day, but my inbox is not a chat tool or a to-do list. Need a quick response? Reach me on Teams.
  • Text or email anytime
    • I understood ideas need to be shared at the moment or they will be forgotten, so feel free to contact me anytime.
  • Try text or email if you want a better/ more detailed response from me
    • I regard my written responses much better than verbal as I am a slow thinker I struggle to explain my thoughts by talking straight out of my mind.

How I prefer to receive feedback 💬

I love all forms of feedback, but I would appreciate it if you keep really really direct and blunt comment private. I am more than willing to address your concerns (and provide solutions) 1:1.

If I don't respond to you... 💤

Rest assured, it's not because I dislike you. It's probably becuase your questions are challenging. If you require a more immediate response please feel free to send me a chaser on Teams or schedule a call.

Things I love 😻

Honesty, transparency and being organised and well-prepared. My aim is to check all these boxes.

Oh, and I love cats.

Things I need 🫂

Trust, personal space and time to think (i am a very slow thinker, which can make me appear unresponsive during debates and discussions).

What Kaho thinks he's good at 💪

Very eager to learn anything new. Good listener. 👍 at ingesting, organising complex information and giving concise summaries.

What Kaho thinks he's not so good at 😰

Communicating the issues I am facing (without thorough preparation). Additionally I am not very talkative and my social awkwardness = 3000.

If you think there's anything I need to improve, please don't hesitate to tell me!
